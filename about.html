Hi, I'm Annette!

I'm a computer science major at Oregon State University with a focus in computer graphics.

Fueled by a deep appreciation for the symbiotic relationship between graphics technology and creativity, I strive to balance strengthening my foundation in computer science and crafting personal narratives with 3D software.

This pursuit has led me towards graduate-level coursework and involvement in projects and research about computer graphics, visualization, and machine learning. In tandem, my curiosity has inspired the creation of 3D animations, providing firsthand insights into the remarkable evolution of CG tools for artists.

My passion for CG has also driven me to contribute to knowledge-sharing initiatives and engage in continuous self-learning.
