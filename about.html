Hi, I'm Annette!

I'm a senior computer science major at Oregon State University with a focus in applied computer graphics.

My interests lie at the intersection of graphics technology and creativity, and I aim to balance building a strong computer science foundation with creating 3D projects that tell stories and explore artistic expression.

This has led me to advanced coursework and hands-on projects in computer graphics, visualization, and machine learning, while developing 3D animations and interactive projects to gain insight into production pipelines and the evolving needs of creative tools.

My passion for CG has also driven me to contribute to knowledge-sharing initiatives and to engage in continuous self-learning.
