  1. 1998 Aibo Commercial (Blender)
    • Created a 3D animated recreation of Sony's 1998 Aibo entertainment robot commercial using self-taught skills in Blender. Demonstrated proficiency in 3D animation, character modeling and rigging, camera layout, lighting, set building, and editing.
  2. [WIP] Funny How Time Slips Away (Blender)
    • Currently working on a personal animation project that's based on Ricky Nelson's rendition of the song "Funny How Time Slips Away." Learning Blender's particle system, rigify, and cloth system.
  3. Chromatic Aberration Shader (C/C++, OpenGL, GLSL)
    • Wrote a chromatic aberration shader with sliders that control individual indices of refraction for R, G, B, allowing for various effects. Referenced this implementation.
  4. Geometry Shader with ChromaDepth (C/C++, OpenGL, GLSL)
    • Wrote a geometry shader that takes the triangles of an object, computes their centroid, quantizes the location of the centroid, and bends the triangle vertices around that centroid to form a partial sphere. More vertices can be added using triangle interpolation. ChromaDepth colors the scene by eye coordinate depth: red in the front, blue in the back, and green in the middle (though this can be adjusted using created sliders that control the Z depths of red and blue).
  5. Cube Mapping Reflective & Refractive Bump-mapped & Displacement-mapped Surfaces (C/C++, OpenGL, GLSL)
    • Wrote a shader that uses cube-mapping to create a reflective and refractive display of a bump-mapped and displacement-mapped surface.
  6. DevDynasty: Full-Stack Ascent (Three.js, Blender, JavaScript, HTML/CSS, Node.js, Express)
    • Collaborated in a team of five to design and develop an interactive web game simulating the full-stack internship application process. Taught myself Three.js and implemented it into the project, allowing for 3D scenes and on-event animations. Built front-end development and backend functionalities using Node.js and Express. Assembled the 3D scene and created animations. This web game was inducted into Oregon State's Web Development Hall of Fame.
  7. Doraemon Coming Out of Nobita's Desk (C/C++, OpenGL)
    • Created a model of Doraemon in a desk using OSU spheres, cylinders, curved line strips, and BoxLists in C/C++ with OpenGL.
  8. Keytime Animation (C/C++, OpenGL)
    • Animated a skeleton driving a car around various objects like the Utah teapot and Stanford bunny in C/C++ with OpenGL.
  9. Step-edged, Blended-edged, & Noisy Elliptical Dots (C/C++, OpenGL, GLSL)
    • Wrote a shader with sliders that control the width, height, blending, and noise of elliptical dots.
  10. Image Manipulation - Fisheye, Whirl, Blend, Mosaic (C/C++, OpenGL, GLSL)
    • Wrote an image manipulation shader toolbox with fisheye, whirl, blend, and mosaic effects.
  11. Random Cobweb Generator (C/C++, OpenGL)
    • Developed a random cobweb generator using C++ and OpenGL for my final project, drawing inspiration from DreamWorks' 2011 paper, "Building and Animating Cobwebs for Antique Sets."
