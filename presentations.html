Presentations

Presentations are both enjoyable and integral to my growth as a graphics enthusiast. I've delved into computer graphics research papers and shared insights with diverse audiences, from graduate students in my research group to high schoolers. I also enjoy sharing what I've learned with friends, exemplified by my passionate speech on the significance of Shrek in exhibiting groundbreaking facial animation techniques. Through these experiences, I not only deepen my own understanding but also work on making complex topics accessible and engaging for others.
