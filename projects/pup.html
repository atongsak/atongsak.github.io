Pixar Undergraduate Program 2024

Maya / Houdini / Katana / Nuke / USD / RenderMan / Blender

During my summer 2024 PUP internship at Pixar, I received training across various departments of the studio's technical pipeline, including modeling, shading, set dressing, layout, lighting, rigging, and FX. I worked with proprietary Pixar software and industry-standard tools such as Maya, Houdini, Katana, Nuke, and USD.

Within a team, I contributed to an animated short by developing a facial rigging system in Blender that automated texture switching, streamlining the animation process and reducing production time. For my individual project, I created a procedural animation workflow in Houdini using VEX, enabling characters to dynamically animate drawing sequences, with results integrated into Blender for final animation.
